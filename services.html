Services We Offer

AQUARIUM SET UP

Are you ready to create your dream aquarium? Our experienced professionals can help! We have been designing and installing beautiful custom freshwater and saltwater aquariums since 2001.

Our Aquarium Set up service includes a free initial consult, custom aquarium design, aquarium installation, and one free follow up service appointment. We will work with you to create a thriving freshwater or saltwater aquarium that you can enjoy for years to come! Please contact us for pricing and more information.

AQUARIUM MAINTENANCE

Let our trained professionals help keep your freshwater or saltwater aquarium healthy and beautiful! Our specialists provide regular maintenance and improve overall health. We have been servicing all sizes of aquariums since 1997.

Our Aquarium Maintenance plans include a free, no obligation initial consult, water testing, equipment testing and maintenance, filter cleaning and replacement, glass or acrylic cleaning, water changes, and saltwater chemicals. We offer weekly, biweekly, and monthly plans. Please contact us for pricing and more information.

FISH SITTING

Going out of town? We have got your fish covered! Our reliable fish sitters have years of experience caring for freshwater and saltwater fish and aquariums of all sizes. We provide scheduled and emergency fish sitting services.

Our Fish Sitting service includes a free, no obligation meeting with one of our fish sitters. During the initial meeting, our sitter will meet your fish and aquarium, discuss your fish sitting needs, and discuss pricing. Many of our fish sitters are also willing to care for other pets. Please contact us for pricing and more information.
